Apps for iPhone and iPad:

Syndicate

A news reader for Google Reader, Twitter and Facebook accounts. Supports iPhone, iPod and iPad. Syncs unread state for Google Reader and remembers last sync position for Twitter and Facebook via iCloud.

Download via App Store.

Phonecard

An app to make phone calls using prepaid calling cards. Enter your card access number, PIN and dial sequence. Call via keypad or contacts list.

Download via App Store.
